March 22, 2020

COVID-19 Update

To our Dog Cabin Family,

We are happy to announce that our regular business hours will remain the same during the Coronavirus shut down. The state of Connecticut has not ordered us to close during this time. We will be taking many precautions to help our employees and customers to stay safe.

We are now offering curbside service for our customers so you don’t have to come inside if you don’t want. Just call our business number (860-704-8080) when you arrive and someone will come out to get your dog. We will also offer this service at the end of the daycare day when you are picking your dog up. But please understand, there are going to be times when we get busy, and there will be a small wait, so please be patient, we will be working as fast as possible! Thank you all for your patronage and were are looking forward to seeing you all soon!
